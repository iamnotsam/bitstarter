Codename: Paincakes

The refreshingly simple interval workout timer

What's the problem?

There are some good interval timers out there, but they are specific to a certain platform, making it difficult for trainers, students, and workout buddies to truly share the workout experience.

Solution?

For this class, I will be building the interval timer I have been craving. I will employ the help of fellow fitness enthusiasts and trainers to define and hone the caliber of timer we all deserve.

What, no fundraising?

I do design and development, and I had a job I love. I don't really want to go further with this concept than to make it awesome. I can do that with the help from my friends who are into working out and who will test and critique the iterations of paincakes.

The needs

  • Easy to see from across the room
  • Audio cues are loud and clear
  • Works well for all major interval schemes like HIIT, Tabata, etc.
  • Attractive design
  • Easily adjust intervals with a few touches
  • Smart defaults reduce time spent touching phone
  • Works the same on Android and iOS devices like iPhone, iPad, and iPod
  • Shows current time clearly
  • Allows pausing/playing, no big deal
  • Easily skip ahead to next exercise or return to previous exercise via simple arrow buttons
  • Customize your interval routine ever how you want
  • Save your customized routines to your library
  • Choose other routines from other libraries
  • Share that you've completed a routine with Twitter or Facebook
  • If two people are really near to one another using the Tabata, the cue sounds should be different
