Paincakes

A web-based tabata timer that doesn't suck

Here is what a good tabata should do

  • Easy to see from across the room
  • Audio cues are loud and clear
  • Works well for all major interval schemes like HIIT, Tabata, etc.
  • Attractive design
  • Easily adjust intervals with a few touches
  • Smart defaults reduce time spent touching phone
  • Works the same on Android and iOS devices like iPhone, iPad, and iPod
  • Shows current time clearly
  • Allows pausing/playing, no big deal
  • Easily skip ahead to next exercise or return to previous exercise via simple arrow buttons
  • Customize your interval routine ever how you want
  • Save your customized routines to your library
  • Choose other routines from other libraries
  • Share that you've completed a routine with Twitter or Facebook
